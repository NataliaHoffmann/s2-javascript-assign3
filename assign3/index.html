    Our Story

    Our restaurant in 1978

    We started our restaurant in 1978, and since then we are always improving. Moving to Canada in 2000 was a big challenge, but we believed in it, and we try our best to become better every day. Our goal is to provide the best pizza of the world, it is the one that our customers deserve.

    Ingredients

    We take care of our ingredients, it is our law

    We are the ones who plant and take care of every ingredient provided by our kitchen. We make sure that every detail of our product is taken care of with love, and more importantly, that we get them on time. Super fresh!

    Family Business

    We take pride in our family business

    The Crippa's Brothers: Enrico, Matteo, and Leonardo Crippa worked hard to make their dreams come true. They believed that bringing their knowledge and experience to America would change many lives.

    It is their lives.
